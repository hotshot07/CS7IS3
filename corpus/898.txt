Title: a survey of buckling theory and experiment for circularconical shells of constant thickness .

Author: seide,p.

Bibliography: nasa tn.d1510, 1962, 401.

Text: a survey of buckling theory and experiment for circularconical shells of constant thickness .  a survey of the state-of-the-artfor the stability of thin-walledconical shells is presented .  knowntheoretical results are summarizedand compared with experiment .  theshortcomings of present knowledgeand recommended work for the futureare discussed .
