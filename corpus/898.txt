a survey of buckling theory and experiment for circularconical shells of constant thickness .  a survey of the state-of-the-artfor the stability of thin-walledconical shells is presented .  knowntheoretical results are summarizedand compared with experiment .  theshortcomings of present knowledgeand recommended work for the futureare discussed .
