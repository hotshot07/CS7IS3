Title: some research on high speed flutter .

Author: garrick, i.e.

Bibliography: 3rd a.a.aero. conf. september 1951. r.a.s. 1952, pp 419-446j .

Text: some research on high speed flutter .paper presents brief discussions of many topics currently of interest in the flutter field . these include /a/ the sonic speed case, /b/oscillating pressure field of propellers, /c/ wing flutter with variousconfigurations including effects of body modes, and /d/ propeller stallflutter .
