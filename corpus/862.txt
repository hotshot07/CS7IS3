the phenomenon of change in buckle pattern in elasticstructures .

stein,m.

nasa tr r39, 1959.

the phenomenon of change in buckle pattern in elasticstructures .  a model is analyzed which exhibits the important propertiesassociated with change in buckle pattern of plates .  the analysis includesa rigorous study of stability in its various modes .  a discussion ofhow the present results may be applied to plates and other elasticstructures is given .
