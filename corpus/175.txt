experiments with static tubes in a supersonic airstream .

holder,d.w., north,r.j. and chinneck,a.

arc r + m 2782, 1953.

experiments with static tubes in a supersonic airstream .  systematic tests have been made at a machnumber of 1.6 on a family of static tubes .  the variableswhich have been investigated are the shape of the nose, thedistance of the holes downstream, and the inclination ofthe tube to the flow .  pressure measurements have also beenmade in the vicinity of a shock wave and close to a wall .
