Title: experimental model techniques and equipment for flutterinvestigations .

Author: molyneux,w.g.

Bibliography: air. eng. 1958.

Text: experimental model techniques and equipment for flutterinvestigations .an outline is given of the uses of flutter models as anaid to the designer in the avoidance of flutter .  detailsare given of the different types and methods ofconstruction that are used for flutter models and of thevarious test facilities that are available for high speedand low speed tests .  the procedure followed in the u.k. for flutterclearance of the full scale aircraft is described, and the valueof the electronic flutter simulator in this field isdiscussed .
