Title: bending tests of ring-stiffened circular cylinders .

Author: peterson,j.p.

Bibliography: naca tn.3735, 1956.

Text: bending tests of ring-stiffened circular cylinders .  twenty-five ring-stiffened circularcylinders were loaded tofailure in bending .  the results arepresented in the form of design curveswhich are applicable to cylinders withheavy rings that fail as a resultof local buckling .
