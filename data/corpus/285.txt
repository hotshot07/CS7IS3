Title: on the flutter of panels at high mach numbers .

Author: hedgepeth,j.m.

Bibliography: j.ae.scs. 23, 1956.

Text: on the flutter of panels at high mach numbers .there have recently arisen some questions as to thepossibility of panel flutter at high dynamic pressures andmach numbers .  in addition, some doubts have been raisedabout the convergence of the galerkin method when applied tosuch problems .  this note is intended to shed light on thesematters .
