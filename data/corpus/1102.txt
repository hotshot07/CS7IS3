Title: a five-stage solid fuel sounding rocket system .

Author: swanson, a.g.

Bibliography: nasa memo 3-6-59 l, 1959 .

Text: a five-stage solid fuel sounding rocket system .a five-stage solid-fuel sounding-rocket system which can boost a payload of 25 pounds to an altitude of 525 nautical miles and that of 100pounds to 300 nautical miles is described . data obtained from a typical flight test of the system are discussed .
