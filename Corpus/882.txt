the variation of gust frequency with gust velocityand altitude .  information on atmospheric turbulence obtained from countingaccelerometer records is examined and relations giving the variation ofgust frequency with gust velocity and altitude are obtained .  theresults are summarized in a form convenient for use in estimating thefatigue life of an aircraft .
