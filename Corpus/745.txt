an automatic method for finding the greatest or leastvalue function .the greatest or least value of a function of severalvariables is to be found when the variablesare restricted to a given region .  a method isdeveloped for dealing with this problem and iscompared with possible alternatives .  the methodcan be used on a digital computer, and isincorporated in a program for mercury .
